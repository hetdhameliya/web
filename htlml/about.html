Who We Are?

The Empire Group is a family of brands and businesses, making it possible for customers around the world to express themselves through fashion and design, and to choose a more sustainable lifestyle. We create value for people and society in general by delivering our customer offering and by developing with a focus on sustainable and profitable growth.

The customer is at the heart of our unique business model, which includes design, production, distribution and sales through our extensive retail network.

Our vision is to design and deliver fashion & lifestyle brands, while always keeping it fresh
